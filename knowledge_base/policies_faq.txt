AcmeCorp Policies and FAQ

Return Policy:
- 30-day money-back guarantee for new customers
- Prorated refunds available for annual contracts (minus setup fees)
- No refunds after 90 days of service

Data Security:
- SOC 2 Type II certified
- GDPR and CCPA compliant
- Data encrypted at rest and in transit (AES-256)
- Regular third-party security audits
- Customer data stored in US-based AWS data centers

Common Questions:

Q: What is the typical implementation timeline?
A: Standard implementations take 4-8 weeks. Complex enterprise deployments may require 12-16 weeks.

Q: Do you offer free trials?
A: Yes, 14-day free trials available for AcmeCRM Pro. ERP trials are case-by-case.

Q: Can I integrate with existing systems?
A: Yes, we provide REST APIs and pre-built connectors for Salesforce, SAP, Oracle, and 50+ other platforms.

Q: What training is included?
A: All packages include 2 days of virtual training. On-site training available for additional fee.

Q: Is there a contract commitment?
A: Annual contracts required for enterprise products. Month-to-month available for AcmeCRM Pro at higher rates.
